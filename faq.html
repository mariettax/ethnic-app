Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I just use Google Maps to find ethnic stores?▶

While Google Maps is great for general searches, it often misses the nuance and specificity required for ethnic and specialty food. Ethnic offers advanced filters (by culture, dietary needs like Halal/Kosher/Vegan), highlights hidden gems, provides community-verified authenticity, and even suggests recipes. How do you verify the authenticity and stock of stores?▶

We rely on a combination of community input, direct outreach to store owners, and our internal verification process. Our goal is to ensure the information provided is as accurate and trustworthy as possible, reflecting the needs and experiences of the diaspora community.

Is Ethnic (Local Food Finder) free to use?▶

Yes, Ethnic is completely free to search and use. We believe that access to authentic cultural food should be easy and accessible for everyone in the community. Our current business model focuses on connecting users with businesses, and we do not charge users for searching.

Can I add a store that's not listed on Ethnic?▶

Absolutely! We encourage our community to help us grow and improve our listings. If you know of a fantastic ethnic market, restaurant, or specialty store that isn't on our platform, please use the "Submit a Store" link in the footer. We'll review your submission and add it to our verified list!

How accurate are the location details and hours?▶

We strive for accuracy and regularly update our store information. However, store hours and inventory can sometimes change without notice. We recommend calling ahead for critical needs. Your feedback helps us keep our information up-to-date!
